The goal of our study was to understand the relationship between mental health and social media activity, specifically looking at sensitivity and response to social reward (likes, replies, reposts) .

Your donated data will be analyzed to assess and quantify whether there is a correlation between your ADHD symptoms and the way you interact with each platform, specifically following the receipt of social reward. For example, we will analyze whether you spend more time on the app after receiving likes, and whether people with higher ADHD symptom severity spend comparatively more or less time on the app following likes. We will also analyze whether symptom severity is correlated with posting frequency, time of posting, and types of posts, and whether this relationship varies with symptoms over time.
